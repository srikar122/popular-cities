Bangalore

While Bangalore (Bengaluru) is perhaps most well-known as a tech hub in India, it truly has so much more to offer! Bangalore is famous for it’s gardens, religious sites, nightlife, shopping and architecture. If you’re traveling to Bangalore for the first time, you’ll have a lot of options to explore!

The capital of Karnataka, Bangalore is in the southern part of India. The city and outskirts have over 8 million residents so it’s full of energy and life!
